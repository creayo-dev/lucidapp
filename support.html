Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to iOS Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → Lucid. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. Lucid doesn't handle billing directly — Apple does.

What is the Autonomy Score?

It tracks your raw cognitive performance across 5 domains based on your exercise results. Higher is better. Most users improve 8–12 points in their first 30 days.

Why didn't I get notifications?

Check Settings → Lucid → Notifications in the iOS Settings app and make sure they're enabled. If you tapped "Not now" during onboarding, see the question below.

I tapped "Not now" in onboarding. How do I turn on reminders?

Open Lucid → Settings → Enable Notifications. Or go to iOS Settings → Lucid → Notifications and turn them on from there.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your exercise data stays on your device and in your private Supabase account. We don't sell data or use it for advertising. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Something is broken. What should I do?

Email support@lucidapp.co with your device model, iOS version, and a description of what happened. Screenshots help. We'll get back to you within 24 hours.
