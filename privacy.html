Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What We Collect

Lucid collects the minimum data needed to provide exercises and track your progress.

Data we collect:

  • Device identifier (anonymous, not tied to your identity)
  • Exercise completion data (which exercises you do, accuracy, time spent)
  • Cognitive scores and streak data
  • Writing responses to prompts (used to improve exercises)
  • Image description responses

We do NOT collect:

  • Your name, email, or phone number (unless you create an account)
  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, or other personal files
  • Browsing history
  • Data from other apps

How We Use Your Data

  • To track your exercise progress
  • To adapt exercise difficulty to your ability level
  • To personalize your feed based on engagement
  • To improve the quality of exercises
  • To generate aggregate, anonymized usage statistics

We never sell your data. We never share individual user data with third parties. We never use your data for advertising.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (hosted on AWS). Exercise data is associated with an anonymous device identifier, not your personal identity.

If you create an account, your email is stored securely and used only for authentication.

Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Database and authentication (supabase.com/privacy)
  • Art Institute of Chicago API: Public domain artwork images
  • Pexels: Stock photography (pexels.com/privacy)
  • Open Trivia Database: Trivia questions (CC BY-SA 4.0)

These services may collect anonymized usage data per their own privacy policies.

Images

Images shown in exercises come from the Art Institute of Chicago (public domain, CC0) and Pexels (Pexels license, free for commercial use). We store image URLs and metadata, not the images themselves. Photographer and source attribution is displayed for all images.

Your Writing

When you complete writing exercises, your responses are stored securely on our servers. We use your responses to: (1) display them back to you within the app, (2) improve exercise quality through aggregate analysis, and (3) improve our exercise models. We will never publish your individual writing publicly or share it with third parties. You may request deletion of your writing data by contacting privacy@lucidapp.co.

Data Retention

We retain your exercise data for as long as you use the app. If you delete the app, your local data is removed. To request deletion of server-side data, contact us at privacy@lucidapp.co.

Children

Lucid is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with data, contact us and we will delete it.

Changes

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be communicated through the app. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests: privacy@lucidapp.co
